
# DS Trainer App — Marketing Overview

Use this overview with ChatGPT (or another tool) to develop positioning, messaging, and go-to-market ideas.

---

# Product in One Sentence

**DS Interview Trainer** is a web app that helps people prepare for data science and analytics interviews by practicing real interview-style questions, getting instant AI feedback, and tracking progress with a scorecard — with a free tier and optional paid access.

---

# Tagline (Current)

Interview-ready in 30 minutes a day. DS interview drill that adapts to you.

---

# Target Audience

- People preparing for **data science**, **analytics**, or **product analytics** interviews.
- Candidates who want structured practice (not just reading) and feedback on their answers.
- Users who prefer short daily sessions (e.g. 30 min) and progress they can see (scorecard, accuracy by topic).

---

# How It Works (User Flow)

1. **Sign up** (email/password; optional invite code).
2. **Get a question** — Matched by topic and difficulty (definition → intuition → applied → technical → hard case).
3. **Submit an answer** — Free-text response.
4. **Get instant feedback** — AI grading (score 0–4), what was good, what was missing/wrong, example answers, optional follow-up question.
5. **Rate the question** (1–5) and optionally **add an insight** (tip or note) for future users.
6. **Track progress** — Scorecard: questions used, points, streak, accuracy by topic. Can filter practice by topic (e.g. Causal, ML, Experimentation, Metrics, Gen AI).
7. **Optional:** Use “Help” for hints; answer follow-up questions for deeper practice.

---

# Core Features (for Messaging)

- **Curated question bank** — Topics: Causal, Machine Learning, Experimentation, Metrics, Gen AI. Categories include Causal Inference, Case Study, Experiment Design, Metrics, Gen AI, Statistics, Product Sense.
- **Difficulty ladder** — Definition → Intuition → Applied product → Technical/Assumptions → Hard case.
- **AI grading** — Immediate score and written feedback (what was good, what was missing, examples).
- **Scorecard** — Progress, streak, accuracy by topic, today’s stats.
- **Topic-based practice** — Choose a topic or get mixed practice.
- **Community feedback** — Rate questions and add insights; admins see ratings and comments to improve content.
- **Free tier** — Cap on free questions (e.g. 10); paid tier for unlimited (Stripe when enabled).
- **Admin** — User management, invites, full question bank management (create, edit, stats, ratings, flags).

---

# Value Propositions (for Copy)

- **Efficiency** — “30 minutes a day” — small, consistent practice instead of cramming.
- **Relevance** — Real interview-style questions (A/B testing, causal inference, metrics, ML, product sense, Gen AI).
- **Feedback** — Not just right/wrong: structured feedback and example answers so you know what to improve.
- **Adaptation** — Questions and difficulty are structured so practice can target weak spots.
- **Progress** — Scorecard and accuracy by topic make improvement visible.
- **No fluff** — Focus on practice and feedback, not long theory lessons.

---

# Differentiation Angles

- **Interview-specific** — Built for DS/analytics interviews (causal, experimentation, metrics, product sense), not generic “data” quizzes.
- **Structured difficulty** — From definition to hard case, so practice can match level and ramp up.
- **AI + human curation** — Questions and feedback are curated; users can rate and add insights so content improves.
- **Lightweight** — Web app, sign up and start; no heavy setup or long courses.

---

# Business Model

- **Free tier** — Limited number of questions (e.g. 10) to try the product.
- **Paid tier** — Unlimited questions (Stripe; can be gated behind invite or open signup).
- **Admin** — Invite codes and user tiers for early access or beta.

---

# Tech / Credibility (Optional for Marketing)

- Web app (Next.js); AI-powered grading; progress and scorecard; topic and difficulty structure; optional “interview-ready in 30 min” framing.
